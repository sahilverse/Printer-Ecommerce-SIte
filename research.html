Research & References

Dribbble

Dribbble is a thriving community platform that showcases creative work, with a focus on web design, graphic design, and UI/UX design. It was an amazing resource for our project, delivering a variety of inspiration and cutting-edge design ideas.

w3schools

W3Schools is an educational website offering comprehensive tutorials and references on web development languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and more. It was quite helpful to me in creating an accordion for our project. The site provided clear, step-by-step instructions and examples, making it easy to understand and implement the accordion feature.

Wix.com

Wix's variety of website designs greatly inspired our blog. Although we didn't use their templates, we gathered many ideas from their styles. This helped us learn about web design trends and effectively mix elements, leading us to create a unique and user-friendly blog.

Holland Cooper

The design components included on Holland Cooper's website served as a big source of inspiration for the creation of our project's "Contact Us" page. The icons utilized were taken from those seen on their website, resulting in a sleek and professional design.
